
Left
$$
A\approx\sum_{i=0}^{n-1}\Delta{x}*f(a+\Delta{x}i)
$$
Right
$$
A\approx\sum_{i=1}^{n}\Delta{x}*f(a+\Delta{x}i)
$$
Middle
$$
A\approx\sum_{i=0}^{n-1}\Delta{x}*f(a+\frac{\Delta{x}}{2}+\Delta{x}i)
$$
Trapezoid
$$
A\approx\frac{1}{2}
$$

---
$[a , b]$
n = # of rects,
$\Delta{x}$ = (b-a)/n
> "midpoint Riemann Sum"
